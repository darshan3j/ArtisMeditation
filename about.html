About Us

Hi, I am Shilpa Bhanushali and welcome to my shop!
My passion is turning walls into captivating spaces.
Stunning Macrame Fiber Art is the ideal addition to the blank walls in your living rooms, hallways, dining rooms, bedrooms, and windows, almost anywhere you can think of.
I love working closely with clients to design and create one of a kind rope art for homes and businesses customized exactly as you desire.

"THE ART OF KNOTTING"


A good piece of art has the ability to completely transform a room with it's unique style and vibrant colors.
At A.I.M we try to balance contemporary & classic by offering a beautiful collection of products to suit every element of your lifestyle.
Using only the finest materials we spend countless hours hand-knotting all of our homeware products until we reach perfection.
